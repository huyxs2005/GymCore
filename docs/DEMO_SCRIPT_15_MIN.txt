GymCore 15-minute demo script

Goal:
- Cover the most important user-facing business flows without rushing.
- Use seeded local accounts and data that already exist in the local SQL scripts.

Environment before demo:
- Seed DB with .\seed-db.ps1
- Start app with .\start-dev.ps1
- Frontend: http://127.0.0.1:5173

Seeded accounts:
- Admin: admin@gymcore.local / Admin123456!
- Receptionist: reception@gymcore.local / Reception123456!
- Coach: coach@gymcore.local / Coach123456!
- Customer: customer@gymcore.local / Customer123456!

Suggested timeline:
- 00:00 - 01:00: intro + login
- 01:00 - 05:00: customer membership + promotions + shop
- 05:00 - 07:00: receptionist pickup confirmation
- 07:00 - 09:00: customer review after pickup
- 09:00 - 12:00: coach + customer progress/PT flow
- 12:00 - 15:00: admin support/goals/insights wrap-up

Part 1 - Customer login and membership/promotion/shop flow

1. Open http://127.0.0.1:5173
2. Click Login
3. Enter:
- Email: customer@gymcore.local
- Password: Customer123456!
4. Click Login

What to say:
- "We start as the customer because this is where membership, PT, and commerce come together."

5. Open Membership from the top navigation.
6. Click the Gym + Coach plan card.
7. If the coupon dropdown is visible, choose MEMBERBOOST.
8. Point at the checkout summary and pricing preview.

What to say:
- "The membership page supports day pass, gym-only, and gym-plus-coach. Coupons are validated before checkout, and the page explains the exact commercial impact before payment."

9. Open Promotions.
10. Find the WELCOME10 promotion card.
11. Click Claim Voucher.
12. In the success modal, mention the wallet update, then click Awesome!.

What to say:
- "Promotions are not just banners. Customers can claim a coupon directly, and it immediately becomes available inside checkout."

13. Open Product Shop.
14. Click Add to cart on the first product card.
15. Click View cart.
16. In cart, select promo code WELCOME10.
17. Point at the discount preview and the Checkout with PayOS button.

What to say:
- "The commerce flow is tied to wallet coupons, and checkout is ready for hosted PayOS payment."

Part 2 - Reception pickup confirmation

18. Logout.
19. Login as receptionist:
- Email: reception@gymcore.local
- Password: Reception123456!
20. Open Pickup Desk.
21. In the queue, select the awaiting pickup invoice.
22. Point at invoice detail, paid items, recipient info, and the Confirm pickup button.
23. Click Confirm pickup.

What to say:
- "Product handoff is confirmed at the front desk. This matters because review eligibility is locked behind pickup completion, not just payment."

Part 3 - Customer review after pickup

24. Logout.
25. Login again as customer.
26. Open Order History.
27. Point out that the picked-up order now shows Leave review.
28. Click Leave review.
29. Keep rating at 5.
30. In Comment, type: Demo review after pickup
31. Click Submit review.
32. Show Edit review, update the comment to: Demo review updated after pickup
33. Click Update review.

What to say:
- "This demonstrates the current business rule clearly: the customer can review only after pickup is confirmed. The review can also be edited later."

Part 4 - Coach writes progress and customer sees it

34. Logout.
35. Login as coach:
- Email: coach@gymcore.local
- Password: Coach123456!
36. Open Customers.
37. Select the first customer in the list.
38. Open the Progress tab.
39. Update:
- Height: 180
- Weight: 79
40. Click Save progress.
41. Open Notes.
42. Pick any session in the first dropdown.
43. Type note: Session pacing improved and squat depth is more stable.
44. Click Add note.

What to say:
- "Coaches can update measurable progress and add session notes from their own workspace. Those updates flow forward to the customer view."

45. Logout.
46. Login as customer again.
47. Open Progress Hub.
48. Point at the latest coach note and latest progress block.
49. Click Open PT dashboard.

What to say:
- "The customer does not have to search for updates. Progress Hub brings together PT context, coach notes, and the latest health/progress signal in one place."

Part 5 - Customer PT dashboard / AI / booking

50. On the PT page, point at the dashboard first.
51. If Book PT Plan is visible, click it, then Open Schedule Planner.
52. Choose Monday and one available slot.
53. Click Save and Search.
54. Point at Fully Match or Partial Match results.

Alternative if an active PT phase is already visible:
- Show Change recurring plan.
- Show My PT Schedule.
- Point out pending/active PT state and exception handling surfaces.

What to say:
- "The PT experience is dashboard-first. The matching preview and the actual booking flow share the same rules, and the AI assistant is aligned with that same matching logic."

Part 6 - Admin support, goals, and coach insights

55. Logout.
56. Login as admin:
- Email: admin@gymcore.local
- Password: Admin123456!
57. Open Support Console.
58. Search for customer@gymcore.local
59. Open the customer detail/result.
60. Point at the consolidated lifecycle view: account, membership, PT, commerce, notifications.

What to say:
- "Support Console is the fastest place for admin staff to understand the full customer lifecycle without jumping blindly across modules."

61. Open Goals.
62. Show one existing goal card.
63. Open Coach Insights.
64. Point at the summary cards, watchlist, and roster/feedback view.

What to say:
- "Admin can manage recommendation goals and also monitor coach load and review quality from separate but connected admin surfaces."

Closing line:
- "In one system, GymCore covers account management, memberships, PT booking, customer progress, product sales, pickup, reviews, staff operations, and admin support workflows with shared business rules across the whole app."
